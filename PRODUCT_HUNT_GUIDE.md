## Submission Process

### Step 1: Create Product
1. Go to https://www.producthunt.com/posts/new
2. Click "Upload a Product"
3. Fill in all the details above
4. Upload your assets (logo, screenshots)

### Step 2: Gallery Images (4-6 images recommended)

**Image 1: Hero/Overview**
- Main interface showing Claude interacting with Anki
- Text: "AI-Powered Anki Assistant"

**Image 2: Feature - Card Generation**
- Screenshot of text input â†’ generated cards
- Text: "Generate flashcards from any text"

**Image 3: Feature - Analytics**
- Statistics dashboard or insights
- Text: "Smart learning analytics"

**Image 4: Feature - Natural Language**
- Examples of commands
- Text: "Control Anki with natural language"

**Image 5: Tech/Architecture**
- Diagram showing MCP â†’ Anki flow
- Text: "55 tools, 9 resources, 10 prompts"

**Image 6: Open Source**
- GitHub stats or code snippet
- Text: "Open source & community-driven"

### Step 3: Launch Timing

**Best times to launch:**
- **Tuesday-Thursday**: Most active days
- **12:01 AM PST**: Product Hunt day starts (gets you full 24h)
- **Avoid Monday & Friday**: Lower engagement
- **Avoid major holidays**

**Schedule your launch:**
- You can schedule up to 2 weeks in advance
- Choose a day with less competition
- Check the upcoming launches calendar

### Step 4: Promotion Strategy

**Before Launch (1 week):**
- Post teasers on Twitter
- Email your network about the upcoming launch
- Prepare your maker story
- Join Product Hunt Ship to build pre-launch momentum

**Launch Day:**
- Share on Twitter with #ProductHunt
- Post in relevant Slack/Discord communities
- Email your mailing list
- Respond to ALL comments quickly (first 6 hours critical)
- Share updates throughout the day

**After Launch:**
- Thank everyone who supported
- Share the results (even if not #1)
- Continue engaging with comments
- Cross-post to other platforms

### Step 5: Hunter (Optional)

Consider asking a well-known "hunter" to post your product:
- Hunters with followers give you initial visibility
- Search for hunters interested in AI/Education/Dev Tools
- Reach out via Twitter DM or email
- Provide them with all materials ready

## Product Hunt Tips

**Do:**
- Respond to EVERY comment within first 6 hours
- Be genuine and helpful
- Share your maker journey
- Ask for honest feedback
- Update your post with feature requests

**Don't:**
- Ask for upvotes directly
- Use fake accounts to upvote
- Spam communities
- Ignore criticism
- Give up if not #1 (top 5 is great!)

## Success Metrics

**Good Launch:**
- 100+ upvotes
- Top 10 of the day
- 50+ comments
- Featured in newsletter

**Great Launch:**
- 300+ upvotes
- Top 5 of the day
- 100+ comments
- Product of the Day/Week

**Viral Launch:**
